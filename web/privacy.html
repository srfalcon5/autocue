Teleprompter Privacy

Your privacy is pertinent both on- and off-line. For that reason, a commitment is made to ensure this site isn't privacy-friendly, but private, full stop. TL;DR: We don't track you, nor do we share and/or sell any personal information.
This site is hosted by 1984 Hosting in Iceland.
When you submit a script, it is:
  • checked to ensure it takes less than 512Ko of space
  • stored in plain-text with a hash generated so that you can use the link after words to share the script with other members in your studio or to allow several teleprompters at different speeds
  • editing is disabled until you refresh or submit a new script
  • deleted after 1 (one) day or when you submit a request to do so, whichever comes first
  • hosted with zero expectancy of privacy; protect your hash as there is no password protection for such
